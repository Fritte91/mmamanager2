December 30th Balance Adjustments

Date: December 30, 2024

Hello Managers,
A new balance update is now live! See the changes below.

GENERIC :

  • Escaping clinch is now slightly harder.
  • Standing punch moves score a little less points (for victory points).
  • High Kicks have slightly lower base values.

TALENTS :

  • Rage fist: Punch damage increased from 10% to 16%, Punch combo chance increased from 10% to 12%.

NEW EFFECT :

In Addition, 3 talents have their effects entirely changed :

  • Mob Brawler: Increase Critical hit chance by 8% in the clinch stance...
  • Frenzied Beating: After the fighter lands a successful hit, increase damage by 10%...
  • Staggering Strikes: Successful critical hits decrease the opponent's initiative by 4 for 5 seconds.

Over the course of 2025, more of the least exciting talents will get similar changes. Happy New Year, may it be filled with many medals and belts!
